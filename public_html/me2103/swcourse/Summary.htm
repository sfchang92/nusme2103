4. Summary

4.1 Summary of the lesson

After going through the e-Learning course on the topic of "Introduction to 3D Solid Modeling (SolidWorks)", users should be confident of modeling simple parts, creating assemblies and produce drawings using SolidWorks. Users should also have a clear understanding of how 3D Solid Modeling is achieved using a CAD software after the course.

4.2 Futher Readings

Users can refer to the textbook "Introduction to 3D Solid Modeling with SolidWorks " published by the author as shown below or refer to "Getting Started Manual" and "SolidWorks Help Topics" from the Help menubar in SolidWorks to further understand the advanced capabilities of SolidWorks.
