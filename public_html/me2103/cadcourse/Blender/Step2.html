Step 2: Creating the Jug

2.1) Click on New and select  to create a new part document

2.2) Select the Front plane (you have to select a plane before you can sketch). Click on Sketch . 

2.3) Using the Line , Circle , 3-point Arc , Smart Dimemsion and Add Relation tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions.

2.4) Click on Revolve , select the axis of revoution for 360 deg. Click on    to finish.

2.5) Base on Front plane and away of 66 mm from the center, using the Line , Circle , 3-point Arc , Smart Dimemsion and Add Relation tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions. Click on    to finish.

2.6) Click on Revolve , select the axis of revoution for 360 deg. Click on    to finish.

2.7) Click on Shell and select the bottom and top surface as shown. Click on to finish.

2.8) Click on Fillet and select the relevent edges as shown. Set fillet radii to 10 mm. Click on to finish.

2.9.1) Based on the Right plane,  using the Line , 3-point Arc , Smart Dimemsion and Add Relation tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions. Click on    to finish.

2.9.2) Based on the Front plane,  using the Line , 3-point Arc , Smart Dimemsion and Add Relation tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions. Click on    to finish.


2.9.3) Using the Sweep with select the projecting profile and path, remider to uncheck the 'Merge result' option. Click on    to finish.

2.10) Using the Intersect with select the both non-merged body, then select the regions to be removed. Click on    to finish.

2.11) Click on Fillet and select the relevent edges as shown. Set fillet radii to 6 mm. Click on to finish.


2.12) Click on Fillet and select the relevent edges as shown. Set fillet radii to 8 mm. Click on to finish.

2.13) You can now save this file by clicking File -> Save. Save this solid part as "Jug".
