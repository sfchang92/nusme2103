Step 3: Creating the Collar

3.1) Click on New  and select    to create a new part document.

CREATING MAIN BODY

3.2) Select the Top plane (you have to select a plane before you can sketch). Click on Sketch . 

3.3) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 74mm.

3.4) Click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Blind and set depth at 27mm. Click on    to finish.

 

3.5) Base on top surface and using the Circle with diameter 60 mm, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions. Then, Click Extruded Cut with Through All. Click on to finish. As shown.

3.6) Base on the same top surface and using the Circle with diameter 69.6 mm, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions. Then, Click Extruded Cut with 12 mm depth. Click on to finish. As shown.
               

3.7) Base on the bottom surface and using the Circle with diameter 69.6 mm, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions. Then, Click Extruded Cut with 10 mm depth. Click on to finish. As shown.

3.8) Based on the top surface,  using the Line , Smart Dimemsion and Add Relation tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions. Click on    to finish.

 

3.9) Click on Extruded Boss/Base with Depth 27 mm. Click on  . As shown.

3.10) Base on front or right plane, sketch the vertivcal straight line Line from the origin and change it to hidden line . As shown.

             

3.11) Base on previous hidden line, use Circular Pattern  with A= 30 deg and 12 time duplication. As shown.

3.12) Click on Draft with 3 degree, then select all the outer surface. Click on    to finish.
                   

3.13)  Base on top surface with PCD of 64 mm diameter, and using theLine , Circle , 3-point Arc , Smart Dimemsion and Add Relation tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions. Click on to finish. As shown.

3.14) Click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Blind and set depth at 12mm. Click on    to finish.
           

 

3.15) You can now save this file by clicking File -> Save. Save this solid part as "Collar".
