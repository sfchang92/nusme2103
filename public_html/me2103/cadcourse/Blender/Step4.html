Step 4: Creating the Upper Blade

4.1) Click on New and select  to create a new part document

4.2) Select the Top plane (you have to select a plane before you can sketch). Click on Sketch . 

4.3) Using the Rectangle  and Smart Dimemsion tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions.

4.4) Click on Extruded Boss/Base and type Depth 1.2 mm. Click on  . As shown.



4.5) On top of new solid surface, Using the Circle  and Smart Dimemsion tool, Sketch a Circle  of diameter 7.85 mm. Then, click on Extruded Boss/Base and type Depth 1 mm. Click on to finish. As shown.

4.6) On bottom of new solid surface, Using the Circle  and Smart Dimemsion tool, Sketch a Circle  of diameter 6.3 mm. Then, Click Extruded Cut with depth of 1.2 mm. Click on to finish. As shown.

4.7) Click on Plane with 135 degree between selective reference face and edge. Click on  . As shown.

4.8)  Based on the newly created plane, using the Line , Smart Dimemsion and Add Relation tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions.

4.9) Base on front plane, sketch the vertivcal straight line Line from the selected egde with 45 degree, and then change it to hidden line . As shown.

4.10) Base on previous hidden line (as projection) and sketch profile, click on Extruded Boss/Base and type Depth 1.2 mm. Click on to finish. As shown.


4.11) Click on Chamfer with Angle distance (D=1.2 mm & A=45 deg) and select the relevent edges as shown. Click on to finish.

4.12) Base on front or right plane, sketch the vertivcal straight line Line from the origin and change it to hidden line . As shown.

4.13) Base on previous hidden line, use Circular Pattern  with A= 180 deg and 2 time duplication . As shown.


4.14) Click on Fillet and select the relevent two top edges and two bottom edges as shown. Set fillet radii to 2.2 mm. Click on to finish.

4.15) You can now save this file by clicking File -> Save. Name this solid model as "Upper Blade".
