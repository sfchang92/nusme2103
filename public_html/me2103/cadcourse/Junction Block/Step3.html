Step 3: Creating the Circular Boss

 

3.1) To create the circular boss: Select the side of the solid model highlighted in green as shown below as the sketching plane.


a. Click on Circle and sketch a circle. Click on Smart Dimension and set its diameter to 44 mm. Position the center of the circle at 76 mm above the bottom of the large square base and 25 mm away from the right side of the sketching plane as shown.


b. Click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Blind and set depth at 39 mm ( 76 -12 - 25 ). Click on to finish.

 

 

 

 

 

 


 

3.2) To create the hole on the circular boss: Select the circular face of the circular boss created as the sketching plane.

a. Click on Circle and sketch a circle sharing the same center as the circular face selected. Click on Smart Dimension and set its diameter to 20 mm.

b. Click on Extruded Cut , select type Blind and set depth at 51 mm ( 39 + 12 ). Click on   to finish.
