Step 4: Creating the Square Boss

 

4.1) Defining the Sketching plane with aid of reference geometry: To create the sketching plane, click on Reference Geometry -> Plane. Select the circular face highlighted in the diagram as 'First Reference'. Change the Distance to 152 mm and make sure the inserted plane is on the opposite side as the circular boss. (Check the Flip box if necessary ). Click on   to finish.

 

     

 

 

 

4.2) To create the square boss: Select the plane created in Step 4.1 as the sketching plane.

a. Select Back View. Click on Rectangle and sketch a rectangle with its top edge coincident with the top edge of the model. Click on Smart Dimension and set its length and breadth to 46 mm. Position the sketch as shown in the diagram below.


b. Click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Up to Surface and select the curved surface highlighted in pink in the diagram below. Click on to finish.

     

 

 

 

 

 

 


 

4.3) To create the hole on the square boss: Select the square face of the boss created in Step 4.2 as the sketching plane.

a. Click on Circle and sketch a circle. Click on Smart Dimension and set its diameter to 20 mm. Position the circle at the center of the square face.

 

 


 

 

 

 

b. Click on Extruded Cut , select type Blind and set depth at 76 mm. Click on   to finish.
