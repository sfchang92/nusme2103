Step 5: Creating the Loft

5.1) Sketching the first circle that defines the loft: Click on Left and select the face as shown below as the sketching plane. Click on Circle and sketch a circle. Click on Smart Dimension and set its diameter to 58mm. 

Position its center 90mm above the base of the entire model and 37 mm ( 25 + 12 ) away from either sides of the body created in Step 1. Click on to exit sketch.


5.2) Sketching the second circle that defines the loft:

a. First, create the sketching plane. Click on  Reference Geometry -> Plane. Under 'First Reference', select the back face of the solid model as shown. Change the Distance to 31 mm and ensure that the inserted plane is away from the model. (Check the Flip box if necessary ).Click on to finish.

   

b. Select the plane created in Step 5.2a as the sketching plane. Click on Circle  and sketch a circle concentric with the 58mm circle sketched in Step 5.1. Click on Smart Dimension and set its diameter to 44 mm. Click on to exit sketch.

5.3) To create the loft:

a. Click on 'Lofted Boss/Base' . Select the 58 mm circle ( from Step 5.1) and the 44 mm circle (from Step 5.2) as the 'Profiles'. (Be careful when u select the circles otherwise the loft will be distorted!). Click on to finish.

         

 

5.4) To create the hole on the loft: Select the circular face of the loft as the sketching plane.

a. Click on Circle and sketch a circle sharing the same center as the circular face of the loft. Click on Smart Dimension and set its diameter to 25 mm.

b. Click on Extruded Cut , select type Blind and set depth at 43 mm ( 31 + 12 ). Click on   to finish.
