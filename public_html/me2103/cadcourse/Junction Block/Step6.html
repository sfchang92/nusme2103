Step 6: Creating the Ribs

6.1)To create the slanted rib:

a. First, create the sketching plane. Click Reference Geometry -> Plane. Select the circular face highlighted in the diagram as 'First Reference'. Change the Distance to 76 mm and make sure the inserted plane lies in the middle of the solid model. (Check the Flip box if necessary). Click on to finish.

     

b. Select the plane created in Step 6.1a as the sketching plane. Select Front View. Click on Line and sketch a line connecting the bottom edge of the body and the upper edge of the square base as shown beloew.

     

c. Click Rib . Select Both Sides and change Distance to 12 mm. If necessary, select Flip material side to ensure direction of rib is inwards. Click on to finish.

     

 

 

 

 

6.2) To create the second rib:

a. First, create the sketching plane. Click Reference Geometry -> Plane. Select the rectangular face highlighted in the diagram as 'First Reference'. Change the Distance to 69 mm and make sure the inserted plane lies in the middle of the solid model. (Check the Flip box if necessary). Click on  to finish.

   

b. Select the plane created in Step 6.2a as the sketching plane. Select Right View. Click on Line and sketch a vertical line connecting the top edge of the base and the mid section of the curved surface.



c. Click Rib . Select First Side and change Distance to 12 mm. If necessary, select Flip material side to ensure direction of rib is inwards. Click on to finish.

     

   

 

6.3) To create the third rib:

a. First, create the sketching plane. Click Reference Geometry -> Plane. Select the face highlighted in the diagram as 'First Reference'. Change the Distance to 57 mm and make sure the inserted plane lies in the middle of the solid model. (Check the Flip box if necessary). Click on to finish.

       

b. Select the plane created in Step 6.3a as the sketching plane. Select Front View. Click on Line and sketch a vertical line connecting the top of the base and the mid section of the curved surface at 13mm from the edge of the base. Click on Smart Dimension and set the distance to be 13mm.


c. Click Rib . Select Second Side and change Distance to 12 mm. If necessary, select Flip material side to ensure direction of rib is inwards. Click on to finish.

      

   

 

6.4) To create the fourth rib:

a. First, create the sketching plane. Click Reference Geometry -> Plane. Select the rectangular face highlighted in the diagram as 'First Reference'. Change the Distance to 63 mm and make sure the inserted plane lies in the middle of the solid model. (Check the Flip box if necessary). Click on to finish.

   

b. Select the plane created in Step 6.4a as the sketching plane. Select Left View. Click on Line and sketch a vertical line connecting the edge of the base and the bottom of the aquare boss.


c. Click Rib . Select Both Sides and change Distance to 12 mm. If necessary, select Flip material side to ensure direction of rib is inwards. Click on to finish.
