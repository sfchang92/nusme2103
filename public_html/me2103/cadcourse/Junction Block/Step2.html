Step 2: Creating the Base

2.1) To create the smaller base: Click on bottom view and select the bottom face as the sketching plane.

a. Click on Rectangle and sketch a rectangle with its left edge coincident with the left edge of the plane. Next, click on Smart Dimension and set the length and breadth of the rectangle to 52 mm. Position the sketch as shown.

     

b. Click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Blind and set depth at 23 mm ( 38 -15 ). Click on   to finish.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 


 

2.2) To create the larger base: Select the bottom face of the smaller base created in Step 2.1 as the sketching plane.

a. Click on Rectangle and sketch a rectangle. Next, click on Smart Dimension to set the length and breadth of the rectangle to 126 mm. Position the rectangle as shown.

         

b. Click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Blind and set depth at 15 mm. Click on to finish.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 


 

2.3) To create one of the small holes on the base: Select the bottom face of the large rectangular face as the sketching plane.

a. Click on Circle and sketch a circle. Next, click on Smart Dimension and set the diameter to 12 mm. Position the circle as shown.

    

b. Click on Extruded Cut , select type Blind and set depth at 15 mm. Click on to finish.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

2.4) To create the rest of the 3 small holes on the base: Since the 4 holes are arranged in a pattern, make use of Linear Pattern .

a. Click on Linear Pattern . Under 'Feature to Pattern', select the hole created in Step 2.3.

b. Under 'Direction 1', select the edge indicated as Direction 1 in the diagram below. Click on Reverse Direction if necessary to ensure the copies are in the correct orientation. Set Number of Instances to 2 and the Spacing as 100 mm.

c. Under 'Direction 2', select the edge indicated as Direction 2 in the diagram below. Click on Reverse Direction if necessary to ensure the copies are in the correct orientation. Set Number of Instances to 2 and the Spacing as 100 mm. Click on to finish.

     

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 


 

2.5) To create the middle big hole on the base: Select the bottom face of the large square base as the sketching plane.

a. Click on Circle and sketch a circle, Next, click on Smart Dimension and set the diameter to 25mm. Position it at the center of the square base as shown in the diagram.

b. Click on Extruded Cut , select type Blind and set depth at 38mm. Click on to finish.
