STEP 4: Creating the Additional Features

 

4.1) To create a hole of diameter 10mm : Select the face highlighted as shown.

a. Click on Circle and sketch a circle.

b. Select Smart Dimension and set diameter to 10mm. Position the circle as shown.


c. Click on Extruded Cut , select Through All. Click on to finish.

 

4.2) To remove a block from the base: Select the front face of the model.

a. Sketch a Rectangle and set the breadth of the rectangle to 5mm and set length to 14mm.

Position it at a distance of 32mm from the edge as shown.


b. Click on Extruded Cut , select Through All. Click on to finish.
 

 

4.3) Removing a block; extruded cut in two directions: Select the plane highlighted as shown.

a. Sketch a Rectangle  and set the breadth to 13mm and length to 50mm.


c. Click on Extruded Cut , select Through All for Direction 1. Check Direction 2 and select Through All for Direction 2. Click on to finish.

 

 
 

 

4.4) To create a hole of diameter 10mm on top: Select the top face of the model. 

a. Click on Circle and sketch a circle of diameter 10mm. Position the circle as shown.

b. Click on Extruded Cut , select Through All. Click on to finish.

 
 

 

4.5) Removing a disc: Select the front face of the model.

a. Click on Circle and sketch a circle of diameter 50mm. Position the circle as shown.

b. Click on Extruded Cut and set depth to 13mm. Click on to finish.


4.6) Extruding a disc: Select the base of the disc.

a. Click on Circle and sketch a concentric circle of diameter 22mm.

b. Click on Extruded Boss/Base and set depth to 5mm. Click on to finish.


 
 

 

4.7) Removing a block: Select the top-most face of the model.

a. Select Rectangle and sketch a rectangle as shown. 



b. Click on Extruded Cut and set depth to 30mm. Click on  to finish.

 
 

 

4.8) Removing a circular portion: Select the front face of the model.

a. Click on Circle and sketch a circle with its center at the corner (shown as a green dot in diagram below).

b. Select Smart Dimension and set the diameter to 54mm.


c. Click on Extruded Cut and set depth to 8mm. Click on to finish.
 

 

4.9) Removing a circular portion, center 3mm from edge: Select the plane highlighted as shown.

a. Click on Circle and sketch a circle of diameter 66mm. Position the center of the circle as shown.


b. Click on Extruded Cut and set depth to 32mm. Click on to finish.

4.10) Extruding a disc: Select the plane highlighted as shown.

a. Click on Circle and sketch a concentric circle. Set the diameter to 15mm.


b. Click on Extruded Boss/Base and set depth to 8mm. Click on to finish.
