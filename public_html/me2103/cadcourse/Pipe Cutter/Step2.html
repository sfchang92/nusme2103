Pipe Cutter

Step 2: Creating the Cross Slider

2.1) Click on New and select  to create a new part document

2.2) Select the Front plane (you have to select a plane before you can sketch). Click on Sketch . 

CREATING THE MAIN BODY

2.3) Using the Line ,  Smart Dimemsion and Add Relation tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions.

2.4) Click on Extruded Boss/Base and type Depth 130 mm. Click on  . As shown.

2.5) Using the Line ,  Smart Dimemsion and Add Relation tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions.

2.6) Click on Extruded Boss/Base  and type Depth 46.8mm from an offset point of 83.2mm. Click on  . As shown.

 

CREATING THE SPECIFIC FEATURES ON THE MAIN BODY

2.7)  To create coutersink holes, click on Hole Wizard with specifying the point location as shown below based on top view. Click on  to finish.

2.8) Continues on the Hole Specification dialogue, click on selection of legacy hole with information of Diameter 6mm, Depth 100mm, C-Sink Angle 90degree and C-Sink Diameter 11mm. Click on  to finish.

2.9) To create Tapped holes, click on Hole Wizard  with specifying the point location as shown below based on side view. Click on  to finish.

2.10) Continues on the Hole Specification dialogue, click on selection of straight tap with applying ANSI Metric Standard size of M8x1.25 and blind of 19.75mm plus thread blind of 16.00mm. Click on  to finish.


 

2.11) You can now save this file by clicking File -> Save. Save this solid part as "Cross Slider".
