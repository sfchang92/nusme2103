Step 6: Creating the Swing Crescent

6.1) Click on New and select  to create a new part document

6.2) Select the Front plane (you have to select a plane before you can sketch). Click on Sketch . 

6.3) Using the Line ,  Smart Dimemsion  and Add Relation  tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions. Then, click on Revolve , select the axis of revoution for 180 deg. Click on    to finish.



6.4) From the Front plane, using the Line ,  Smart Dimemsion  and Add Relation  tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions. Then, click on Extruded Cut , select set Through All. Click on    to finish.

 

6.5) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 8mm. Then, click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Blind and set depth at 12mm. Click on    to finish.

6.6) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 8mm. Then, click on Extruded Cut , select type Blind and set depth at 12mm. Click on    to finish.

6.7) Using the Line ,  Smart Dimemsion  and Add Relation  tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions.

6.8)  Click on Extruded Boss/Base and type Depth 10 mm. Click on  . As shown.

6.9) On top surface, create hidden lines with specific angle and intersect at the origin as shown below for reference on next step. 

5.10) Reference to the newly hidden line, Click on Hole Wizard and pick the end point of the hidden. Continues on the Hole Specification dialogue, click on selection of Straight Tap with applying ANSI Metric Standard size of M5x0.8 and blind of 12.4mm plus thread blind of 10.00mm. Click on  to finish.


6.11) Use Circular Pattern  with A= 21.3 deg and 9 time duplication. As shown.

6.12) To create Tapped holes, click on Hole Wizard with specifying the point location as shown below based on side view. Click on  to finish.

6.13) Reference to the newly hidden line, Click on Hole Wizard  and pick the end point of the hidden. Continues on the Hole Specification dialogue, click on selection of Straight Tap with applying ANSI Metric Standard size of M8x1225 and blind of 19.75mm plus thread blind of 16.00mm. Click on  to finish.

6.14) To create simple holes, click on Hole Wizard  with specifying the point location as shown below based on side view. Click on  to finish.

6.15) Continues on the Hole Specification dialogue, click on selection of legacy hole with information of Diameter 6.2mm, Depth 100mm, C-bore Diameter 10mm and C-Bore Depth 6mm. Click on  to finish.

6.16) To create simple holes, click on Hole Wizard  with specifying the point location as shown below based on side view. Click on  to finish.

6.17) Reference to the newly hidden line, Click on Hole Wizard  and pick the end point of the hidden. Continues on the Hole Specification dialogue, click on selection of Straight Tap with applying ANSI Metric Standard size of M8x1225 and blind of 19.75mm plus thread blind of 16.00mm. Click on  to finish.

6.18) You can now save this file by clicking File -> Save. Name this solid model as "Swing Crescent".
