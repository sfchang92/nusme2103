Step 3: Creating the Cross Feed Screw

3.1) Click on New  and select    to create a new part document.

CREATING MAIN BODY

3.2) Select the Top plane (you have to select a plane before you can sketch). Click on Sketch . 

3.3) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 10mm. Then, click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Blind and set depth at 15mm. Click on    to finish.

 

3.4) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 12mm. Then, click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Blind and set depth at 102mm. Click on    to finish.

3.5) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 10mm. Then, click on Extruded Boss/Base  , select type Blind and set depth at 20mm. Click on    to finish.
 

3.6) Click on Chamfer with Angle distance (D=1 mm & A=45 deg) and select the relevent edges as shown. Click on  to finish.

 

3.7) Based on the top surface, using the Helix/Spiral with applying constant pitch of height 6mm and pitch 1mm. Click on    to finish.

 

3.8) Redo step 3.7 for another end with height 11mm and pitch 1mm. Click on  . As shown.

3.09) To create haxegon profile, using the Polygon with 6 sides and applying A/C 10mm. Then,  click on Extruded Boss/Base  , select type Blind and set depth at 20mm. Click on    to finish.

  

3.10) You can now save this file by clicking File -> Save. Save this solid part as "Cross Feed Screwr".
