Pipe Cutter

Step 9: Creating the Gear Box Cover

9.1) Click on New and select   to create a new part document

9.2) Select the Top plane (you have to select a plane before you can sketch). Click on Sketch . 

 

CREATING THE MAIN BODY

9.3) Using the Line , 3 Point Arc , Smart Dimemsion and Add Relation tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions.

9.4) Click on Extruded Boss/Base and type Depth 16 mm. Click on  . As shown.

9.5) Using the Line , Trim , 3 Point Arc , Smart Dimemsion and Add Relation tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions.

 
9.6) Click on Extruded Boss/Base and type Depth 11 mm. Click on  . As shown.

CREATING THE SPECIFIC FEATURES ON THE MAIN BODY

9.7) On the top surface of the solid, Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 35mm. Then, click on Extruded Cut , select type Blind and set depth at 11mm. Click on    to finish.

9.8) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 26mm. Then, click on Extruded Cut , select type Blind and set depth at 3mm. Click on    to finish.

9.9) To create couterbored holes, click on Hole Wizard with specifying the point location as shown below and select the relevent edges as shown. Click on to finish.

9.10) Continues on the Hole Specification dialogue, click on selection of legacy hole with information of Diameter 6.2mm, Depth 100mm, C-bore Diameter 10mm and C-Bore Depth 6mm. Click on to finish.

 

9.11) You can now save this file by clicking File -> Save. Name this solid model as "Cross Slide Mt".
