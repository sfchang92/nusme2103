Step 8: Creating the Retainer Half Ring

8.1) Click on New and select  to create a new part document

8.2) Select the Front plane (you have to select a plane before you can sketch). Click on Sketch . 

8.3) Using the Line ,  Smart Dimemsion  and Add Relation  tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions. Then, click on Revolve , select the axis of revoution for 180 deg. Click on    to finish.




8.4) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 3mm. Then, click on Extruded Boss/Base  , select type Blind and set depth at 7.5mm. Click on    to finish.

8.5) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 3mm. Then, click on Extruded Cut , select type Blind and set depth at 10mm. Click on    to finish.




8.6) On top surface, create hidden lines with specific angle and intersect at the origin as shown below for reference on next step.

8.7) Reference to the newly hidden line, Click on Hole Wizard and pick the end point of the hidden. Continues on the Hole Specification dialogue, click on selection of legacy hole with information of Diameter 5mm, Depth 8mm, C-Sink Angle 90degree and C-Sink Diameter 10mm. Click on  to finish.

8.8) Base on front or right plane, sketch the vertivcal straight line Line from the origin and change it to hidden line . As shown.

 

8.9) Base on previous hidden line, use Circular Pattern  with A= 30 deg and 6 time duplication. As shown.

8.10) You can now save this file by clicking File -> Save. Name this solid model as "Retainer Half Ring".
