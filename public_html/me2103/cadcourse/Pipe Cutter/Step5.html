Step 5: Creating the Fix Cast Crescent II

5.1) Click on New and select  to create a new part document

5.2) Select the Front plane (you have to select a plane before you can sketch). Click on Sketch  . 

5.3) Using the Line ,  Smart Dimemsion  and Add Relation  tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions. Then, click on Revolve , select the axis of revoution for 180 deg. Click on    to finish.

5.4) Sketching on top of the surface, using the Line ,  Smart Dimemsion  and Add Relation  tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions.



5.5) Click on Extruded Boss/Base with applying bi-direction. The first direction is 18mm and the second direction is Up to Vertex. Click on  . As shown.

5.6) Using the Intersect with select the both non-merged body, then select the regions to be removed. Click on    to finish.

 

5.7) Click on Plane with 41mm offset from the origin top plane.. Click on  . As shown.

5.8)  Based on the newly screated plane, sketching a 45 degree line by using the Line ,  Smart Dimemsion  and Add Relation  tool.

5.9) Click on Rid and select Both Sides and change Distance to 10mm. If necessary, Flip material side to ensure direction of rib is inwards. Click on    to finish.

5.10) Click on Mirror  with selecting the right plane to dupulicate the newly rib to another side. As shown.


 

5.11) Using the Line ,  Smart Dimemsion  and Add Relation  tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions.

5.12) Click on Extruded Boss/Base and type Depth 12 mm. Click on  . As shown.

5.13) To create Tapped holes, click on Hole Wizard with specifying the point location as shown below based on side view. Click on  to finish.

5.14) Continues on the Hole Specification dialogue, click on selection of Simple Hole with applying ANSI Metric Standard size of diameter 10mm. Click on   to finish.

5.15) On top surface, create hidden lines with specific angle and intersect at the origin as shown below for reference on next step. 

5.16) Reference to the newly hidden line, Click on Hole Wizard  and pick the end point of the hidden. Continues on the Hole Specification dialogue, click on selection of Straight Tap with applying ANSI Metric Standard size of M6x1.0 and blind of 6mm plus thread blind of 12.00mm. Click on  to finish.

5.17) Base on front or right plane, sketch the vertivcal straight line Line from the origin and change it to hidden line . As shown.

 

5.18) Base on previous hidden line, use Circular Pattern  with A= 30 deg and 6 time duplication. As shown.

 

5.19) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 8mm. Then, click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Blind and set depth at 15mm. Click on    to finish.

5.20) To create Tapped holes, click on Hole Wizard with specifying the point location as shown below based on side view. Click on  to finish.

5.21) Continues on the Hole Specification dialogue, click on selection of legacy hole with information of Diameter 12mm, Depth 200mm, C-bore Diameter 10mm and C-Bore Depth 14.5mm. Click on  to finish.

5.22) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 8mm. Then, click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Blind and set depth at 30mm. Click on    to finish.

5.23) To create Tapped holes, click on Hole Wizard with specifying the point location as shown below based on side view. Click on to finish.

5.24) Continues on the Hole Specification dialogue, click on selection of Straight Tap with applying ANSI Metric Standard size of M10x1.5 and blind of 24.5mm plus thread blind of 20.00mm. Click on 

 

5.25) Using the Line ,  Smart Dimemsion and Add Relation tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions. Then, click on Extruded Cut , select set Through All. Click on     to finish.

5.26) On the same top surface, Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 70mm. Then, click on Extruded Cut , select set Offset of 84mm and set  Blind depth of 50mm. Click on     to finish

5.27) Reference to the newly hidden line, Click on Hole Wizard and pick the end point of the hidden. Continues on the Hole Specification dialogue, click on selection of Straight Tap with applying ANSI Metric Standard size of M6x1.0 and blind of 15mm plus thread blind of 12.00mm. Click on  to finish.

 

5.28) Create hidden lines with specific angle and intersect at the origin as shown below for reference on next step. 

 

5.29) By using same plane of step 5.28, Sketching on top of the surface, using the Line ,  Smart Dimemsion and Add Relation tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions.



5.30) Click on Extruded Boss/Base with applying bi-direction. The first direction is 15mm and the second direction is 15mm. Click on   . As shown.

5.31) To create Tapped holes, click on Hole Wizard with specifying the point location as shown below based on side view. Click on  to finish.

5.32) Continues on the Hole Specification dialogue, click on selection of Straight Tap with applying ANSI Metric Standard size of M16x2.0 and blind of 38mm plus thread blind of 32.00mm. Click on  to finish.

5.33) Click on Mirror  with selecting the right plane to dupulicate the newly profile to another side. As shown.

5.34) Click on Fillet and select the relevent edges as shown. Set fillet radii to 3 mm. Click on  to finish.

 

5.35) You can now save this file by clicking File -> Save. Name this solid model as "Fix Cast Crescent II".
