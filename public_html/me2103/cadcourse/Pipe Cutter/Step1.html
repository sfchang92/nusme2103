Pipe Cutter

Step 1: Creating the Cross Slide Mt

1.1) Click on New and select   to create a new part document

1.2) Select the Front plane (you have to select a plane before you can sketch). Click on Sketch . 

 

CREATING THE MAIN BODY

1.3) Using the Line ,  Smart Dimemsion and Add Relation tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions.

1.4) Click on Extruded Boss/Base and type Depth 120 mm. Click on  . As shown.

1.5) Using the Line ,  Smart Dimemsion and Add Relation tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions.

 
1.6) Click on Extruded Boss/Base and type Depth 50 mm. Click on  . As shown.

CREATING THE SPECIFIC FEATURES ON THE MAIN BODY

1.7) To create couterbored holes, click on Hole Wizard with specifying the point location as shown below and select the relevent edges as shown. Click on to finish.

1.8) Continues on the Hole Specification dialogue, click on selection of legacy hole with information of Diameter 6.6mm, Depth 100mm, C-bore Diameter 10mm and C-Bore Depth 10mm. Click on to finish.

 

1.9) On the top surface of the solid, Using the Line ,  Smart Dimemsion and Add Relation tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions. Click Extruded Cut with depth of 28 mm. Click on to finish. As shown.


1.10) Using the Circle  and Smart Dimemsion tool, Sketch a Circle  of diameter 24 mm. Then, Click Extruded Cut with depth of 24 mm. Click on to finish. As shown.

1.11) Using the Circle  and Smart Dimemsion tool, Sketch a Circle  of diameter 15 mm. Then, Click Extruded Cut with option of Up to Next. Click on to finish. As shown.

1.12) Using the Circle  and Smart Dimemsion tool, Sketch a Circle  of diameter 24 mm. Then, Click Extruded Cut with 9mm. Click on to finish. As shown.


1.13) Using the Circle  and Smart Dimemsion  tool, Sketch a Circle  of diameter 15 mm. Then, Click Extruded Cut  with option of Up to Next. Click on  to finish. As shown.

1.14) Click on Fillet and select the relevent edges as shown. Set fillet radii to 3 mm. Click on to finish.


 

1.15) You can now save this file by clicking File -> Save. Name this solid model as "Cross Slide Mt".
