Pipe Cutter

Step 10: Assembly and Sub-Assemblies

This CAD product is relatively complex compared with previous practise model. This section is introduce how to create main assembled pipe cutter with different minor sub-assemblies. This is aid to simplify and manage a complex CAD design systematically.

10.1) Creating sub-assembly of Cutting Device

10.1.1) Click on New and select  to create a new assembly.

10.1.2) Under "Part/Assembly to Insert", click on Browse and select A07 Cross Slide Mt.SLDPRT. Click anywhere on the right window to place the part. This action will set the part "A07 Cross Slide Mt" as the base for the assembly, i.e. it is fixed and cannot be moved.
10.1.3) To impart the Bottom into the assembly: Click on Insert Component , Browse and locate A09 Cross Feed Screw.SLDPRT. Click in the right window to place the Cross Feed Screw.

10.1.4) Click on Mate . Select the cylindrical surface of the Cross Feed Screw and Cross Slide Mt as shown. Select  Concentric . Click on    when done.

(Note: Click on Rotate View , Zoom In/Out , Zoom to Area and/or Pan to rotate, zoom in/out and/or move the model to aid in the selection of the faces.)

10.1.5) Select the flat surface of Cross Feed Screw and Cross Slide Mt as shown. Select Coincident . Click on    when done.
              

10.1.6) Click on Mate . Select the flap surface of the Cross Feed Screw and Thrust Bearing 51100 as shown. Select Coincident  . Click on    when done.

10.1.7) Select the cylinder surface of the Cross Feed Screw and Thrust Bearing 51100 as shown. Select Concentric  . Click on    when done.

10.1.8) Repeat action 10.1.7 for another Thrust Bearing 51100.

10.1.9) Click on Mate . Select the flap surface of the Thrust Bearing 51100 and M10 Hex Nut as shown. Select Coincident . Click on    when done.

10.1.10) Select the cylinder surface of the  M10 Hex and Cross Feed Screw as shown. Select Concentric . Click on    when done.



10.1.11) Repeat action 10.1.7 for another M10 Hex Nut.

10.1.12) Click on Mate . Select the flap surface of the Cross Feed Screw and Cross Slider Handle as shown. Select Coincident . Click on    when done.

10.1.13) Select the flap surface of the Cross Feed Screw and Cross Slider Handle as shown. Select Coincident  . Click on    when done.  Then repeat again for another corrresponding flat surface.

10.1.14) Click on Mate . Select the cylinder surface of the Cross Feed Screw and Feeder Block as shown. Select  Concentric . Click on    when done.


10.1.15) Select the flap surface of the Cross Feed Screw and Feeder Block as shown. Select Parellel . Click on    when done. 

10.1.16) Click on Mate . Select the flap surface of the Cross Slider and Cross Slide Mt as shown. Select  Coincident . Click on    when done.

10.1.17) Select the cylinder surface of the Cross Slider and Feeder Block as shown. Select Concentric . Click on    when done.

10.1.18) Click on Mate . Select the flap surface of the Cross Slider and Parting Cutter as shown. Select  Coincident . Click on    when done.

10.1.19) Select the flap surface of the  Cross Slider and Parting Cutter as shown. Select Coincident  . Click on    when done.

10.1.20) Select the flap surface of the  Cross Slider and Parting Cutter as shown. Select Coincident  . Click on    when done.

Final assembly product as shown.

10.1.21) You can now save this file by clicking File -> Save. Save this assembly as "SubAss_CuttingDevice".

10.7) Creating the main assembly

10.7.1) Click on New  and select  to create a new assembly.

10.7.2) Under "Part/Assembly to Insert", click on Browse and select Fix Cast Cresent Hub. Click anywhere on the right window to place the part. This action will set the part "Fix Cast Cresent Hub" as the base for the assembly, i.e. it is fixed and cannot be moved.
10.7.3) To impart the Bottom into the assembly: Click on Insert Component , Browse and locate Retainer Half Ring Hub.SLDPRT. Click in the right window to place it.

10.7.4) Click on Mate . Select the cylindrical surface of the Fix Cast Cresent Hub and  Retainer Half Ring Hub as shown. Select  Concentric  . Click on    when done.


10.7.5) Click on Mate . Select the the Fix Cast Cresent Hub and  Retainer Half Ring Hub as shown. Select  Coincident  . Click on    when done.

10.7.6) Click on Mate . Select the cylindrical surface of the Fix Cast Cresent Hub and  Retainer Half Ring Hub as shown. Select  Concentric  . Click on    when done.


10.7.7) Click on Mate . Select the cylindrical surface of the Fix Cast Cresent Hub and  Brass Wear Cresent as shown. Select  Concentric  . Click on    when done.


10.7.8) Click on Mate . Select the the Fix Cast Cresent Hub and  Brass Wear Cresent as shown. Select  Coincident  . Click on    when done.


10.7.9) Click on Mate . Select the cylindrical surface of the Fix Cast Cresent Hub and Swing  Cresent Hub as shown. Select  Concentric  . Click on    when done.


10.7.10) Click on Mate . Select the the Fix Cast Cresent Hub and Swing  Cresent Hub as shown. Select  Coincident  . Click on    when done.


10.7.11) Hide the Fix Cast Cresent Hub, then click on Mate . Select the cylindrical surface of the  Swing  Cresent Hub and  240T Spur Gear as shown. Select  Concentric  . Click on    when done.

10.7.12) Click on Mate . Select the the Swing  Cresent Hub and  240T Spur Gear as shown. Select  Coincident  . Click on    when done.


10.7.13) Click on Mate . Select the the Fix Cast Cresent Hub and Air Motor as shown. Select  Coincident  . Click on    when done.

10.7.14) Click on Mate . Select the cylindrical surface of the Fix Cast Cresent Hub and Air Motor as shown. Select  Concentric  . Click on    when done. Repeat this step for another hole.



10.7.15) Click on Mate . Select the the Drive Shaft and Air Motor as shown. Select  Distance with 20mm. Click on    when done.


10.7.16) Click on Mate . Select the the Drive Shaft and Air Motor as shown. Select  Coincident  . Click on    when done. Repeat this step for surface.


10.7.17) Click on Mate . Select the cylindrical surface of the Fix Cast Cresent Hub and Bearing 7205 as shown. Select  Concentric   . Click on    when done.

10.7.18) Click on Mate . Select the the Drive Shaft and Air Motor as shown. Select  Coincident  . Click on    when done.

10.7.19) Click on Mate . Select the cylindrical surface of the Drive Shaft and 21T Spur Gear as shown. Select  Concentric  . Click on    when done.

10.7.20) Click on Mate . Select the the Drive Shaft and 21T Spur Gear as shown. Select  Coincident  . Click on    when done.

10.7.21) Click on Mate . Select the the Drive Shaft and 21T Spur Gear as shown. Select  Coincident  . Click on    when done.

10.7.22) Click on Mate . Select the the Drive Shaft and Bearing 7202 as shown. Select  Coincident  . Click on    when done.

10.7.23) Click on Mate . Select the cylindrical surface of the Drive Shaft and 21T Spur Gear as shown. Select  Concentric  . Click on    when done.

10.7.24) Click on Mate . Select the cylindrical surface of the Gear Box Cover and Bearing 7202 as shown. Select  Coincident  . Click on    when done.

10.7.25) Click on Mate . Select the cylindrical surface of the Gear Box Cover and Bearing 7202 as shown. Select  Concentric  . Click on    when done.

10.7.26) Click on Mate . Select the cylindrical surface of the Gear Box Cover and Bearing 7202 as shown. Select  Concentric  . Click on    when done.

10.7.27) Click on Mate . Select the flat surface of the Swing Cresent Hub and Cutting Device as shown. Select  Concentric  . Click on    when done.

10.7.28) Click on Mate . Select the flat surface of the Swing Cresent Hub and Cutting Device as shown. Select   Concentric  . Click on    when done.

10.7.29) Click on Mate . Select the cylindrical surface of the Pre-assembled Pipe Cutter and Demo Pipe as shown. Select  Concentric  . Click on    when done.]

10.7.30) You can now save this file by clicking File -> Save. Save this assembly as "Pipe Cutter".
