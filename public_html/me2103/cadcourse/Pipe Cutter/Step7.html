Pipe Cutter

Step 7: Creating the Brass Wear  Crescent

7.1) Click on New and select  to create a new part document

7.2) Select the Front plane (you have to select a plane before you can sketch). Click on Sketch . 

7.3) Using the Line ,  Smart Dimemsion  and Add Relation  tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions. Then, click on Revolve , select the axis of revoution for 180 deg. Click on    to finish.




7.4) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 3mm. Then, click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Blind and set depth at 10mm. Click on    to finish.

7.5) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 3mm. Then, click on Extruded Cut , select type Blind and set depth at 14mm. Click on    to finish.


7.6) You can now save this file by clicking File -> Save. Name this solid model as "Brass Wear Crescent".
