Step 3: Creating the Shaft

3.1) Click on New  and select    to create a new part document.

CREATING MAIN BODY

3.2) Select the Right plane (you have to select a plane before you can sketch). Click on Sketch . 

3.3) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 30mm.

3.4) Click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Blind and set depth at 4mm. Click on    to finish.

 

3.5) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 20mm.

3.6) Click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Blind and set depth at 86mm. Click on    to finish.

               

3.7) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 16mm.

3.8) Click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Blind and set depth at 20mm. Click on    to finish.
           

3.9) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 10mm.

3.10) Click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Blind and set depth at 20mm. Click on    to finish.

             

3.11) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 16mm.

3.12) Click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Blind and set depth at 58mm. Click on    to finish.
                   

3.13) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 10mm.

3.14) Click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Blind and set depth at 17mm. Click on    to finish.
           

3.15) Click on Chamfer and select the two end edges with D 0.5mm and angle of 45 degree as shown.  Click on to finish.

CREATING KEY SLOTS
3.16) Click Plan at centre of the body with offset distance of 5mm.

3.17) Make sure of the created plane and using Straight Slot to create below two featues.

           

3.18) Click Extruded Cut  . Select Type Through all. Click on to finish.

   

 

3.16) You can now save this file by clicking File -> Save. Save this solid part as "Shaft".
