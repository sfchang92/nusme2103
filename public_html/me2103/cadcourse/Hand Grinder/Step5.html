Hand Grinder

Step 5: Creating the Handle

5.1) Click on New and select  to create a new part document

5.2)Select the Right plane (you have to select a plane before you can sketch). Click on Sketch . 

5.3) Using the Line , 3 Points Arcand Smart Dimemsion tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions.


          

5.4) Click on Revolve 360 deg with selected axis . Click on  . As shown.


5.5) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 8mm.

5.6) Click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Blind and set depth at 20.5mm. Click on    to finish.

5.7) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 5mm.

5.8) Click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Blind and set depth at 5mm. Click on    to finish.

5.9) Click on Chamfet and select the relevent edges as shown. Set fillet angle to 45 deg and distance of 0.5 mm. Click on to finish.





5.10) You can now save this file by clicking File -> Save. Name this solid model as "Handle".
