Step 2: Wheel Flange

2.1) Click on New and select  to create a new part document

2.2) Select the Top plane (you have to select a plane before you can sketch). Click on Sketch . 

2.3) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 25mm.

2.4) Click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Blind and set depth at 14mm. Click on    to finish.

2.5) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 50mm.

2.6) Click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Blind and set depth at 4mm. Click on    to finish.

2.7) Click on Circle   and sketch a circle of diameter 40mm.
2.8) Click on Extruded Boss/Base , select type Blind and set depth at 2mm. Click on    to finish.

2.9) For key slot, Using the Line , Circle  of radius 8 mm, Trim  and Smart Dimemsion tool, sketch the profile as shown with its dimensions.

2.10) Click Extruded Cut  . Select Type Through all. Click on to finish.

2.11) Click on Fillet and select the relevent edges as shown. Set fillet radii to 3 mm. Click on to finish.


2.12) You can now save this file by clicking File -> Save. Save this solid part as "Wheel Flange".
