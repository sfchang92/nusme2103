Step 3: Mirror Entities

 3.1) Sketch a horizontal Centerline  starting from the origin to the right with length of 60 mm. From its end point, sketch another vertical Centerline  upwards with height of 6 mm.

     

3.2) From the end point of the 2nd centerline obtained from Step 3.1,

a. Sketch a horizontal Line  to the left with length 25 mm.

b. Sketch a vertical Line  upwards with height 12 mm.


New - Use of Mirror Entities to produce a mirror image of selected lines about an axis.

3.3) Hold Ctrl and select the 2 lines sketched in Step 3.2.

   

3.4) Click on Mirror Entities . Click on the box under "Mirror about" and select the 60mm centerline created. Click on  to finish.
